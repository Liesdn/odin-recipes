Cyberpunk 2077's Johnny Silverhand

An old fashioned with beer and chilli garnish

Time: 5 minutes; 1 serving.

Truly an atomic drink from the legend himself, Johnny Silverhand, like they serve it in the Afterlife.

Ingredients

  • simple syrup, 12ml
  • angostura bitters, 1 dash
  • orange bitters, 1 dash
  • tequila, 60ml
  • dark, high alcohol level beer, 90ml
  • chilli powder

Instructions:

  1. Prepare a glass by rimming it with chilli powder.
  2. In a mixing glass combine everything except the beer.
  3. Stir over ice and strain into the prepare glass.
  4. Using the back of a spoon float the beer. Enjoy!
