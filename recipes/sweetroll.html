Skyrim's Sweetroll

Let me guess, someone stole your sweetroll?

Time: 45 minutes plus cooling time; 15 servings.

Cute little puffy sweets. Warning: might get stolen.

Ingredients for the sweetrolls:

  • salt 10g
  • milk 60ml
  • flour 250g
  • butter 225g
  • 5 eggs
  • vanilla extract 15ml
  • granulated sugar 350g

Ingredients for the glaze:

  • butter 55g
  • milk 30ml
  • confectioner's sugar 250g
  • vanilla extract 5ml

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven at 180Â°C.
  2. Mix the butter and the sugar and beat until fluffy.
  3. Slowly add the eggs and the milk.
  4. Add the flour, salt and vanilla extract and stir gently until mixed.
  5. Pour into greased swell roll pans and bake until golden, about 20 minutes. Allow to cool.
  6. For the glaze, melt the butter and add in the milk.
  7. Slowly mix in the powdered sugar and vanilla. Drizzle over cooled sweet rolls.
